RED

All I see is red.
How your metaphors bled
and I thought of what you said
when I read what your eyes meant,
how it screams, "I want death",
but you smiled and spoke, "red
is the color of your lips,
something that I wont kiss
because I'm afraid that I'll miss".
I wonder, if you'll ever say
what you really meant
everytime you say that I'm heaven sent,
because I know that in my arms, you only feel resent.
I would kiss you,
and bleed my heart out for you,
I know that you would too,
but sometimes I know, you get tired, you do.
and I miss you, it's true
when I cannot see your colors peeking through
this dark cloud that's raining upon you,
I want you to see red,
I want you to know that it's okay
that your metaphors bled,
I want you to know
that it's going to be alright
even if somethimes you get tired,
even if there are no rainbows,
atleast the sun still shines.
I want you to know that it's alright,
that I can see red when I look at you,
that I can see hope in you.
Even if only heaven knows what you really meant,
I want to see you look at me and see it too,
that bright color of hope that I see in you.
-Cholo Perez
