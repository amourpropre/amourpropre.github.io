A KNOCK ON THE DOOR

how many knocks do i have to make on your door?
its been years and i still havent laid my feet down on your floor.

I smell the warm aroma of coffee inside your house.
I heard that you love books so I brought you my favorite.
I am wearing my new green sweatshirt, i hope that you'll like it.
When will you let me enter?
I've been wet from all of the storms,
cold from all of the winters,
I had so many splinters that I got when I tried to carve our names on that big tree outside your house.
I made tallymarks in my hand. I was counting the days til you let me in. I wonder, why wont you let me in?
One night, I saw you on the roof.
You were looking at your scars.
They were healed but I can still see the faint lines in your wrists.
How i wish that I could tell you about the same scars in my hands that i got from waiting for you.
You were trying to reach for the stars and then you slipped over the roof.
I was down there, looking at you from above.
You were like an angel from high above.
You lost your wings and you cant fly,
but im telling you, if ever you'll need one, I'll break my own wings and give it to you.
My wings will fly you to wherever you want to go.
I'll be your wings to be there to protect you and fly you to the moon.

The next moment I knew, you were already in my arms.
You thanked me and asked me in for some coffee.
Finally, the warmth that I couldnt get for free.
I gave you my book, my favorite book.
It's not in the best condition but it's usable.
We talked about how much you love God,
how much you love your mother,
how much you love your home.
I was waiting for you to talk about how much you love me.
But you never did.
So I walked away,
away from you.
Away from your house that I never even liked in the first place.
Here comes the storm,
here comes the winter,
deep wounds from splinters.
But I seized every moment with you.
Like warmed lemon, I squeezed every love for you.
But you never loved me.

And one night, I got tired of walking,
and all that I wanna do is to go back to your home
and ask you to heal my soul.
and when I knock, I'll be safe and sound
in the arms of someone that I dont even know that much.
Please let me in.
Please, a second chance.

Knock knock knock.
Knock Knock knock.
Knock And knock and knock.
And I knocked so much that I hurt my hands.
and I slammed the fucking door
slammed the fucking window.
Where are you now?
where are you now?
And I knocked till I knocked the shit outta my head,
you'll never be with me.

Here comes the storms and the winters,
my wounds and all of my splinters,
and I still cant understand,
I still cant understand.
Honey, why cant you open the door?
I've knocked 500 times but why cant you open the fucking door? 
-Cholo Perez
